About This Assessment

This quiz is based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), which categorizes personalities into 16 distinct types. You'll answer questions that explore four key dimensions:

  • Energy Direction: Extraversion (E) vs Introversion (I)
  • Information Processing: Sensing (S) vs Intuition (N)
  • Decision Making: Thinking (T) vs Feeling (F)
  • Lifestyle Approach: Judging (J) vs Perceiving (P)

Take your time with each question and choose the answer that feels most natural to you. There are no right or wrong answers - only insights into your unique personality!
